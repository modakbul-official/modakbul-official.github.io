Modakbul – Your Campus, Your Community

Modakbul is a community platform designed by students, for students. Find your friends, share schedules, and stay connected on campus.

What is Modakbul?

Modakbul (meaning 'Bonfire' in Korean) is a platform that helps students connect with their campus community. Whether it's finding study buddies, syncing schedules, or sharing events, Modakbul brings students closer together.

Key features

Find Friends

Easily discover classmates and see their schedules.

Plan Events

Organize study groups, social meetups, and campus events

Sync Schedules

See who’s free and when for meetups, studying, or casual hangouts.

Agora Posts

Share your thoughts, ideas, or questions with the campus community. Users can engage by liking, disliking, and replying to posts, creating an open forum for discussion.

Why Modakbul?

For Students, By Students: As students ourselves, we know the struggles of keeping in touch and staying organized. Modakbul is built with you in mind, making your campus life easier and more fun.

Community Focus: Connect with your campus in a meaningful way – make new friends, stay informed about campus events, and never miss out on important moments.
